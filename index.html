About the Programme
Who we are and what we offer
IESCO Scholarship in Malaysia (ISM) is an initiative by the International Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization (IESCO) to attract students from less fortunate places to pursue advanced academic studies in Malaysia. International students are welcomed to apply for this scholarship and further their studies in the selected Malaysian universities.
How Do New Students Apply?
Four steps to securing your IESCO Scholarship
Requirement 1 — Get your offer letter ⌄
Apply at one of the selected universities for the programme of your choice and get the offer letter and VISA approval letter (VAL).
Requirement 2 — Register on ISM ⌄
Once you receive your Letter of Offer, proceed to IESCO Scholarship in Malaysia (ISM) website and register yourself.
Requirement 3 — Await review ⌄
Wait for the reply of approval or disapproval of your application.
Requirement 4 — Receive confirmation ⌄
Once you are approved, the International Educational Scientific and Cultural Organization will contact you for further details.
